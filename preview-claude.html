Key Insight — 2025 NYTS

Four regime changes in six years, and the fastest brand takeover the NYTS has ever seen.

The youth e-cigarette market has rewritten itself every eighteen months: JUUL (2019–2020) → Puff Bar (2021–2022) → Elf Bar (2023–2024) → Geek Bar (2025). Geek Bar wasn't even in the 2024 top five, yet captured 64.1% of youth users in 2025. Only two of the 2025 top ten hold FDA PMTA authorization. Disposables now account for 66.3% of all e-cig device types — up 10.7 points year over year.
